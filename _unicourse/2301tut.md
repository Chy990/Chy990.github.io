$$
\sum M_A=6000\times 0.5+3000\times 3\times \frac{1}{2}\times 2-4\cdot F_B=0
$$

$$
F_B=3000N
$$

$$
F_A=6000+3000\times 3\times \frac{1}{2}-3000=7500N
$$

$$
\sum F_{y}=0
$$
